Help

Vectors: Vectors are mathematical objects used to represent data in multi-dimensional space. Each vector consists of multiple components or dimensions.

Cosine Similarity: Cosine similarity is a measure used to calculate how similar two vectors are. It ranges from -1 (completely dissimilar) to 1 (completely similar). It is computed using the cosine of the angle between two vectors.

How to Use This App

  • Type your text into the input field and press Enter or click Submit.
  • Your query and its vector embedding will be plotted on the graph.
  • Click on any query in the list to select it and see its cosine similarity with other queries.
  • Hover over the points on the graph to see the cosine similarity values.
